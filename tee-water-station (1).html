Our Technology

7-Stage Purification Process

Every drop passes through seven rigorous stages before reaching your home — engineered for absolute safety.

1
🪨
Sediment Pre-Filter

Removes sand, rust, silt and suspended solids down to 5 microns.

2
🪵
Activated Carbon Block

Absorbs chlorine, chloramines, VOCs and odour-causing compounds.

3
🧲
KDF Redox Media

Removes heavy metals — lead, mercury, arsenic — and inhibits bacteria.

4
💡
Reverse Osmosis Membrane

0.0001 micron membrane removes 99.9% of dissolved salts and contaminants.

5
⚗️
Post Carbon Polish

Final carbon filter enhances taste and removes any residual odour.

6
☀️
UV Sterilization

254nm UV light destroys 99.99% of bacteria, viruses and pathogens.

7
🧊
Ozonation & Mineralisation

Ozone sterilises the tank; minerals restore calcium, magnesium and pH 7.2.
